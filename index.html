Our Story

From the Tablelands to your table

Vincenzo's Coffee Lounge is a much-loved family-friendly café in the heart of Mareeba — right in the Atherton Tablelands, one of the only true coffee-growing regions in Australia. It's fitting, then, that great coffee sits at the centre of everything we do.

We're known for our warm atmosphere, friendly service and generous portions. Wherever we can, we source fresh fruit, vegetables and eggs from local Tablelands growers, so every plate tastes like the region it comes from.

Locals, tradies, travellers and families all find their way to our tables — for a strong long black before work, a relaxed breakfast with the kids, or a quick lunch between errands. Whatever brings you in, you'll be looked after.
